Platform Crafting Dzen

Crafting is the extraction of a coin due to its growth. When the price of a coin rises, the profits of the people who have it increase.

The principle of the platform is very simple. With the help of crafting, you get 10% of the accruals from the average weekly growth of Dzen (if the coin has negative growth or does not have it, then the crafting percentage is 1%). For example, if a coin grew by 20% against USDT, then the owner of Dzen receives 2% of USDT, equivalent to the balance of Dzen.
